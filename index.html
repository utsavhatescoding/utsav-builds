About me

Curious about why things work—and how they could work better.

My background is in economics and business. My work sits where research, data, storytelling and product-building meet.

I enjoy taking complex subjects—trade data, market shifts, policy questions or travel information—and making them easier to understand and use.

I’m especially interested in building useful digital tools from Nepal: products that are locally grounded, publicly valuable and simple enough to be used without a manual.
